Shaping Professionals for
India’s Insolvency Ecosystem

The Post Graduate Insolvency Programme (PGIP) — India's premier curriculum for the next generation of insolvency professionals.

Admissions Open 2026 Apply Now

"Intersection of law, finance, and policy..."

It is my privilege to introduce the Post Graduate Insolvency Programme (PGIP) – MA/LLM in Insolvency Batch of 2025-2027 at the National Law University Delhi. Conceived as a pathbreaking initiative, this programme is a reflection of NLU Delhi’s enduring commitment to excellence, innovation, and professional leadership in legal education.

Envisioned to meet the growing demand for highly trained insolvency and restructuring professionals in India and abroad, and designed in collaboration with the IBBI, the PGIP offers an unparalleled academic and practical foundation.

Prof. (Dr.) G. S. Bajpai Vice Chancellor, National Law University Delhi

"Understand the problem first. The law will follow..."

The Post Graduate Insolvency Programme is designed to build deep institutional and professional capacity in insolvency law and practice.

At a time when economic governance is increasingly shaped by complex financial distress and regulatory design, this programme emphasizes rigorous doctrinal grounding, applied problem-solving, and systemic thinking. I look forward to welcoming participants who seek to shape the future of India’s insolvency ecosystem with intellectual rigor and practical competence.

Dr. Raghav Pandey PGIP-LLM/MA Course Director

Program Highlights

ICC

Internship Coordination Committee

The Internship Coordination Committee acts as the vital bridge between students and top-tier firms. It manages structured recruitment drives, ensuring every scholar secures a placement that aligns with their professional aspirations and academic strengths.

Podcast

The Haircut Files

The Haircut Files is a pioneering student-led initiative that bridges complex legal judgments with living history. By unpacking the human and commercial realities behind major insolvency verdicts, this series brings dry courtroom logic to vibrant life.

Students

Future Insolvency Professionals

The PGIP cohort represents the future leadership of India’s insolvency landscape. Rigorously trained in legal, financial, and policy frameworks, these professionals are uniquely equipped to navigate and resolve the most complex commercial distress.

Immersion

Experiential Learning Field Visits

Experiential learning is central to the PGIP pedagogy. Through structured industrial immersions and field visits, scholars move beyond the classroom to observe real-world operational challenges, fostering deep contextual understanding and practical insight.

Community

Student Achievements and Sports

Life at PGIP extends beyond academics, emphasizing resilience, leadership, and teamwork through sports. Active participation in co-curricular events builds character and strengthens the community, ensuring a holistic developmental experience.

Engagement

IBBI Annual Day Engagement

Scholars engage directly with the regulatory heartbeat of India through exclusive forums like the IBBI Annual Day. This exposure offers a front-row seat to high-level policy discourse, connecting students with the architects of the insolvency ecosystem.
